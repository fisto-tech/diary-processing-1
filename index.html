Industrial Infrastructure

State-of-the-Art Dairy Infrastructure

Explore the specialized departments that make Milknest a pioneer in automated, safe, and efficient dairy processing.

01

Milk Collection

Equipped with automatic milk analyzers and rapid-cooling silos to lower raw milk temperatures immediately to 4°C upon arrival, inhibiting microbial activity.

02

Quality Laboratory

Advanced laboratory conducting chemical and microbiological examinations including antibiotic residue, adulteration, and somatic cell count analyses.

03

Processing Plant

Featuring advanced pasteurizers, clarifiers, and homogenizers integrated with a PLC system that regulates temperatures and pressure ratios continuously.

04

Packaging Unit

Aseptic filling lines using medical-grade packaging materials to maximize shelf-life and retain fresh dairy elements without external preservatives.

05

Cold Storage

Walk-in blast chillers and massive cold rooms operating under computerized thermal logging systems to keep products in pristine structural condition.

06

Distribution Network

A GPS-monitored fleet of refrigerated trucks (reefers) configured with remote temperature telemetry ensures uninterrupted cold chains from plant to store.

Corporate Advantages

Why Partner With Milknest?

We combine years of food technology expertise with advanced infrastructure to support your commercial requirements.

01

Advanced Technology

Our SCADA-integrated processing plant utilizes telemetry systems to configure flow speeds, pasteurizing temperature bands, and homogenizing ratios automatically.

02

Strict Quality Control

We follow systematic inspection cycles at multiple checkpoints. Over 50 standard chemical, bacteriological, and somatic parameters are verified before approval.

03

Food Safety Standards

Milknest complies strictly with international hygiene frameworks. Our cleanroom facilities operate under positive air pressure and micro-filtration arrays.

04

Efficient Logistics

Our distribution relies on a proprietary fleet of temperature-logged vehicles that maintain raw milk and ready inventory within strict cold limits continuously.

05

Scalable Production

High-speed machinery combined with vast raw-milk storage silos gives us the operational flexibility to fulfill substantial institutional bulk supplies easily.

06

Trusted Distribution

Servicing major regions via dedicated distribution nodes, wholesalers, and retail operators. We guarantee consistent inventory schedules throughout the year.
